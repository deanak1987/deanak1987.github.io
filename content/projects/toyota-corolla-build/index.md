A comprehensive documentation of restoring and modifying a 1987 Toyota Corolla FX16 GTS for autocross competition, combining automotive passion with technical documentation.

## Overview

This project documents the complete restoration and performance build of my 1987 Toyota Corolla FX16 GTS, originally a rare Japanese market vehicle. The build focuses on transforming it into a competitive autocross machine while maintaining its classic appeal.

## Key Features

### Restoration
- **Mechanical** - Engine rebuild 

### Performance Modifications
- **Engine** - Long-Rod Silvertop 20v 4AGE comprised of the following:
    - Toda 11.5:1 81.25mm forged pistons, 288deg cams, valve springs, oil pump gear
    - MRP 136.6mm forged billet connecting rods
    - MRP billet crank girdle
    - Port and Polished cylinderhead by Dan Steinhart 


### Documentation
- **Photo Gallery** - Before/after shots and build progress
- **Technical Specs** - Detailed part lists and specifications
- **Maintenance Logs** - Ongoing service and modification records
